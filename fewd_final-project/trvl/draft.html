// day 02

art cafe hemingway

the moment you step into this little cafe, you're immediately transported into another world. with hemingway-inspired decor, i'm sure he would have approved the food served here.

must try: eggs benedict hemingway, moroccan iced tea

// day 03

waimea canyon

dubbed "the grand canyon of the pacific." if a picture is worth 1000 words, then take a look at the pictures :) pro-tip: go straight to the waimea canyon lookout if you aren't much of a hiker.

// day 04

kauai coffee company

a native coffee company based in the southside of the island offers free tours at their plantation. you learn about how the kauai coffee company grow their coffee beans, how it's distributed, and after all that learning, you get (unlimited) free samples of their coffee! p.s. don't store your coffee in the freezer/fridge.

must try: banana nut creme coffee

// day 05

java kai

a small breakfast place serving up interesting plates with punchy coffee -- not to mention funny names e.g. crois-sean-wich. java kai is quite popular amongst the locals. great for groups, but watch out for those lines after 9am!

must try: tropical waffles, mac nut latte
